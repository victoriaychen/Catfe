About

The Catfé was founded in 2020 when we decided to combine our two favorite things:

coffee and cats.


The Catfé provides a unique kind of comfort and relaxation that can’t be found anywhere else on the Tufts University campus. We saw students missing their pets from home and wanting a fluffy friend without the added responsibilities, so we knew just how to help them out. Need a study break? Need to destress? This is the perfect place for you.
