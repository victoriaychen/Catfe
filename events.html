Events

Catfé hosts a range of events all year round, including
but not limited to seasonal, celebrational, and holiday events.
Come join us and our fur babies!

Cookie Fiesta

Create mini portraits of our catfé family in cookie form! A perfect afternoon snack but perhaps too good to eat. Best cookie wins an event ticket of choice!

Event time: Sundays 3:00pm-7:00pm

Catfé Brewing

Enjoy a relaxing afternoon learning to make all types of coffee brews with, of course, our cats! No they won't be doing the brewing but smelling is just as wonderful.

Event time: Wednesdays 2:00pm-5:00pm

Cat Backing

Bring your fur baby with you on an outdoors adventure! We will travel together in a group carrying individual cat carriers while admiring the Medford scenery. A favorite event of many!

Event time: Mondays 3:00pm-5:00pm

Movie Night

A fan of scary movies? Well our cats are too, really! Come join us to watch some horror movie classics and other not so scary ones too. We're open to movies suggestions!

Event time: Fridays 8:00pm-10:30pm

Gaming club

Virtual people are great company but we'd argue there's nothing quite like a cat's companion. You bring your laptop, we'll have our cats and delicious snacks!

Event time: Saturdays 9:00pm-11:00pm

Latté Cat Art

Why? Cause there can never be too much cats (or too much coffee). Come learn to make the cutest foam art ever! P.S We even have cat models for reference.

Event time: Sundays 10:00am-11:30am

Cat-o-ween

It's witchy season! And we all know it won't be complete without our mystical furfriends. Come dressed up as witches and enjoy spooky delights with the purrfect company.

Event time: Halloween week

Kittens Greet & Meet

Catfé has recently welcomed the litter of (insert) and (insert). Come meet our newest and, in many's opinion, cutest members of our catfé fur family!

Event time: Fridays to 4:00pm-6:00pm
