Covid Updates to Catfé Rules

Due to Covid-19, we have adjusted policies for the safety of our guests, staff and cats. We have limited the number of guests to 10 at any given time and guests must maintain some social distancing and wearing masks while not consuming food or beverages.


It is advised to come with friends from the same suite or room as distancing will not be required. Please kindly make a reservation online to visit us.
